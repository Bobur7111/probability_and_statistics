Why this step matters

What happens after mapping

Once the correct columns are selected, the analyzer can transform raw operator data into structured statistical outputs.

  • 1 The time column is converted into daily intervals: 00-06, 06-12, 12-18, 18-24.
  • 2 The duration column is grouped into categories such as 0-60, 60-300, 300-600, and 600+.
  • 3 The system builds the joint frequency table nij and joint probability table pij.
  • 4 Marginal distributions, expectation, variance, standard deviation, covariance, and correlation are computed.
  • 5 Charts and an automatic textual conclusion are displayed in the result dashboard.
Statistical Core

Main formulas used next

Joint probability

pij = nij / n

Each cell of the joint frequency table is divided by the total number of valid observations.

Expected value and variance

M[X] = Σ xiP(X = xi)
D[X] = Σ (xi - M[X])²P(X = xi)

The same logic is used for Y after categorical coding of duration groups.

Covariance and correlation

Cov(X,Y) = ΣΣ (xi - M[X])(yj - M[Y])pij
r(X,Y) = Cov(X,Y) / (σ(X)σ(Y))

These indicators determine the direction and strength of the relationship between time category and duration category.
